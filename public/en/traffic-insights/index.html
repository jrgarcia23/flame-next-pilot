People counting

Measure footfall with precision across hours, days, entrances, and areas. Identify which access points contribute most to traffic and uncover behavioral patterns using AI. Predict visitor flows, detect peak hours and days, and compare performance across multiple locations using absolute, relative, and historical data. Maximize the value of your asset and quantify the impact of marketing actions and promotions on visitor volume.

  • Line In & Out counting
  • Staff exclusion (Tag, app, Exclusion zones, IoT
  • Button, Visit duration)
  • Loitering & U turns near entrance detection
  • Group Counting
  • Zones counting

Occupancy

Monitor live occupancy across your spaces and react instantly to crowd levels. Identify trends, anticipate peak times, and automate actions through integration with signage and people counting systems. Stay compliant, improve efficiency and enhance safety across all locations.

  • Real-time occupancy monitoring
  • Occupancy alerts
  • Mobile app monitoring
  • Digital signage integration

Street Traffic

Measure external footfall to understand how many people pass by your location and how many actually step inside. Evaluate the impact of your storefront, improve window attraction, and boost capture rates with data that brings the outside into focus.

  • Passer-by traffic measurement
  • Storefront and window attraction analysis
  • Capture rate (from passer-by to visitor conversion)

Demographics

Gain anonymous insights into the gender and age distribution of your visitors. Use this data to personalize experiences, adapt content dynamically and increase conversion through targeted engagement.

  • Gender detection
  • Age group classification
  • Digital signage integration

Conversion

Connect visitor analytics with sales data to reveal your full conversion funnel — from passer-by to purchase. Monitor key metrics like capture rate, conversion rate and average ticket, by location. Compare performance and turn insight into revenue-driving action.

  • Sales integration
    Conversion funnel analysis
  • Sales conversion rate tracking
  • Capture rate (passer-by to customer)

Vehicle Counting

Track vehicle flow to understand access patterns, optimize parking and logistics, and link car traffic with in-store visits. Ideal for malls, retail parks and large venues aiming to improve efficiency and enhance the overall visitor experience.

  • Vehicle count and flow tracking
  • Entry and exit pattern analysis
